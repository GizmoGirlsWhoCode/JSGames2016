Instructions

  1. Player 1: Choose the heart (coeur) or awl (alene) and click on the chosen symbol.
  2. Player 1: Click a square to claim it, and it will turn red.
  3. Player 2: Click the other symbol (heart or awl).
  4. Player 2: Click a blank square to claim it.
  5. Player 1: Click heart/awl, click a square to claim it. Player 2: click the other, click a square. Repeat this until someone has 3 in a row.
  6. Click Reset to start over or play again.
